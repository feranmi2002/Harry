# Harry Characters
## _Harry Potter Series characters at your fingertips_
### The Harry App is an Android app that helps you know the Harry Potter characters and the details About them.


## Built with the following

#### - Jetpack Compose
#### - Kotlin
#### - MVVM 
#### - Retrofit
#### - Coil
#### - Navigation
